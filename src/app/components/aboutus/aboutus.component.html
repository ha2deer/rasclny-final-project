Our Website Overview

This Project Aims To Reduce Food Waste By Collecting Unused Food From Homes And Restaurants And Providing Users With Money In Return. It Contributes To Raising Environmental And Economic Awareness In Society By Efficiently Utilizing Leftover Food, Reducing Waste, And Supporting Families And Restaurants With Additional Income.

We Have Other Features Such As Taking Glass, Wood, Metal, And Paper In Exchange For Money As Well.
